About

I am a Masters student at The University of Texas at Austin majoring in Computer Science. Currently I am interning at Meta as a SWE-ML intern working on improving account and interest recommendations. In Spring 2025, I worked with Dr. Aditya Akella and Dr. Sanjay Shakkotai on solving exciting problems in using learned representations of network state.
